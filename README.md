# 🍫 Chocolate Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes chocolate sales data using Power BI to uncover insights related to product performance, sales distribution, discounts, salespersons, and country-wise sales trends. The dashboard provides an interactive view of key business metrics, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Identify top-performing products.
- Evaluate the impact of discounts on sales.
- Compare sales across different countries.
- Measure salesperson contributions.
- Track marketing and packing costs.
- Analyze sales channels (Online, Retail, Wholesale).

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI – Dashboard Development & Visualization
- Microsoft Excel/CSV – Data Source
- Power Query – Data Cleaning & Transformation
- DAX – Calculated Measures and KPIs

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1. KPI Cards
The dashboard highlights three key performance indicators:

- Total Sales: 102.57M
- Marketing Spend: 19.09M
- Total Packing Cost: 1.18M

---

### 2. Sales by Product
A pie chart showing the contribution of each chocolate product to total sales.

#### Key Insights:
- 70% Dark Bar generated the highest sales.
- Mixed Assortment and Truffle Gift Box were among the top-selling products.
- Product sales distribution helps identify best-selling items.

---

### 3. Average Amount by Product
A line chart displaying the average sales amount for each product.

#### Insights:
- Mixed Assortment recorded the highest average sales value.
- Premium products generally showed higher average transaction values.

---

### 4. Sales by Salesperson
A bar chart ranking salespersons based on total sales generated.

#### Insights:
- A few salespersons contribute a significant portion of total revenue.
- Useful for performance evaluation and incentive planning.

---

### 5. Discount on Product
A horizontal bar chart showing total discounts provided across products.

#### Insights:
- 70% Dark Bar received the highest discounts.
- Helps analyze whether discounts are driving sales growth.

---

### 6. Country-wise Product Sales
Visualizes product sales across countries.

#### Top Performing Countries:
- Australia
- Brazil
- Germany

This analysis helps identify high-performing markets and expansion opportunities.

---

### 7. Sales Channel Analysis
Interactive filter allowing users to analyze sales by:

- Online
- Retail
- Wholesale

Users can dynamically explore performance across different sales channels.

---

### 8. Geographic Analysis
A map visualization showing sales distribution across countries and regions.

#### Benefits:
- Identifies geographical sales concentration.
- Supports regional marketing strategies.

---

## 📈 Business Insights
- Total sales exceeded 102 Million, indicating strong market demand.
- 70% Dark Bar is the most significant revenue contributor.
- Australia emerged as the strongest-performing market.
- Discount-heavy products should be monitored to ensure profitability.
- Sales performance is concentrated among a few top salespersons.
- Channel analysis enables targeted sales strategies.
